A lot of good work in 2026 happens without a plane ticket. We're headquartered in Mumbai, but the majority of our clients are outside it — in Delhi NCR, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Pune, Chennai, Ahmedabad, and a growing number of Tier-2 cities. The delivery model is fully remote, the outcomes are the same, and for most SMBs that's the right trade.

## Pan-India delivery, the practical version

- **Same time zone.** Everyone's on IST, so no 2am stand-ups, no "we'll look at it first thing our morning."
- **Weekly check-ins on your schedule.** Most clients pick a fixed 30-minute slot — ours or yours — for a standing review.
- **Async communication via Slack or WhatsApp.** Whatever fits how your team already works. We won't force you onto a new tool.
- **All deliverables land in your accounts.** Figma, GA4, Search Console, Meta Ads Manager, Google Ads, GTM — you own everything from day one. No "ask the agency for access" games.

## Indian-market fluency

- **INR invoicing with GST.** Standard 18% GST, proper tax invoices for every engagement. Book under SAC 998314 / 998361 as relevant.
- **Payment terms that match Indian cash-flow realities.** 40/40/20 on projects; monthly in-advance on retainers. Bank transfer, UPI, or corporate card.
- **Creative that doesn't feel translated.** Our writers, designers, and strategists are Indian-market natives. We know the difference between Bangalore D2C tone and Delhi B2B tone without a briefing document.
- **Platform knowledge specific to India.** Swiggy/Zomato economics, JioMart, Meesho, MagicBricks, Practo, IndiaMART, Udaan — the ecosystem your business actually lives inside.

## How engagement typically works

1. **30-minute discovery call.** You tell us the problem. We tell you honestly whether we're a fit.
2. **Scoped proposal** — flat fee for Launch, Revamp, or a clearly-defined project; monthly retainer for Grow; bundled for Launch + Grow.
3. **Kickoff within 5 working days** of signed scope.
4. **First deliverable in week 1** — a discovery doc, a wireframe, or a campaign brief, depending on engagement type.
5. **Weekly rhythm** until launch, then monthly post-launch for retainers.

No account managers in the middle. You'll speak directly to the person doing the work.

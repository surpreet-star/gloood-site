## The brief

Almari — a contemporary Indian apparel brand — needed their Shopify storefront to do more than list SKUs. Their Instagram was doing the heavy lifting for discovery; the site was where sales quietly dropped off. Low add-to-cart, high bounce on collection pages, slow product detail load on mobile.

## What we did

A custom Shopify theme designed around how shoppers actually browse apparel on a phone:

- **Editorial collection pages** — full-width hero per collection, styled model imagery, lookbook-style scrolling that matches how they post on Instagram
- **Fast PDPs** — size grids that don't require a click to reveal, inline size guide, variant switching with persistent image gallery
- **Smart filtering** — filter by fit, occasion, and size without reload; inventory-aware so nothing sold-out shows up as available
- **Pincode checker on PDP** — delivery timeline visible before checkout, no surprises
- **Checkout polish** — Shop Pay, Razorpay UPI, cash-on-delivery toggle (critical for D2C apparel in India)

## Results

In the first 60 days post-launch:

- **Add-to-cart rate 3.1× higher** than the previous theme
- **Checkout conversion +44%** — mostly attributable to the pincode checker reducing cart-to-checkout drop-off
- **LCP 2.1s** on mobile from 4.8s — bounce on collection pages halved

Instagram is still where the brand lives. The site is now where it converts.

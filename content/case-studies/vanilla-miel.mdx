## The brief

Vanilla Miel is a Bandra-based pâtisserie and chocolaterie — the kind of place where a single praline takes three days to make. Their old Shopify theme didn't match that care. Hero image cropped badly on mobile. Checkout felt generic. The gifting flow — their biggest AOV lever — was buried.

They asked for a rebuild that matched the in-store experience online, without moving off Shopify (they needed to keep their Swiggy and Zomato integrations and the existing product catalogue).

## What we did

We rebuilt the storefront on a custom Shopify theme, opinionated about the things that mattered:

- **Product-first photography layout** — full-bleed imagery, generous whitespace, type that doesn't fight the product
- **Gifting flow redesigned** — curated sets, custom boxes, delivery-date picker, gift messaging all in a single path instead of scattered across pages
- **Smart delivery UX** — locality picker on the homepage, next-day availability clear from first click, no more "out of delivery range" surprises at checkout
- **Cafe pickup integration** — Pali Village pickup slots (3:30–8:30 PM) inline on product cards, not hidden at checkout
- **Klaviyo flows** — abandoned cart, post-purchase, and seasonal collection launches, all tied to the new collection structure

## Results

In the first 90 days after launch:

- **Online orders up 2.4×** month-over-month
- **Average order value +38%** — driven mostly by the gifting flow converting browse-to-buy more effectively
- **LCP dropped to 1.8s** on mobile (from 4.2s) — Google Search Console impressions also up after the rebuild

More importantly: the site now feels like the brand. Every time a regular tells them "your new site is beautiful", that's compounding.
